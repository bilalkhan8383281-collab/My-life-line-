Tumhare Naam

Meri jaan,

Jab se tum meri zindagi mein aayi ho, sab kuch thoda zyada khoobsurat lagne laga hai. Har subah tumhare khayal se shuru hoti hai aur har raat tumhare naam par khatam.

Mera baccha, tumhe pata hai — tumhari fikar karna, tumhari chhoti chhoti baatein sunna, yeh sab mujhe sabse zyada sukoon deta hai. Tum jab hasti ho na, toh lagta hai jaise sab kuch thik ho gaya.

Meri pyari si aankhon wali, in aankhon mein hi toh meri poori duniya basi hai. Jitna tumhe dekhta hoon, utna kam lagta hai.

Aur meri pyari si biwi — haan, abhi se yeh keh kar bulata hoon, kyunki mera dil toh yehi maan chuka hai — tumhare bina meri kahani adhoori hai. Tum meri "Kokab" ho, mera sitara, jo hamesha meri raaton ko roshan karta hai.

Meri moti, bas itna jaan lo — kitni bhi door ho, kitna bhi waqt beet jaaye, mera pyaar tumhare liye kabhi kam nahi hoga.

— hamesha tumhara
